Moving from Financial to Coding

I have found this article on a guy who studied to become a Chartered Financial Analyst.
He faced a difficult decision become a Chartered Financial Analyst or learn to code on a website called FreeCodeCamp.
He spent 4 years studying at a university and graduated with honors but why he gave it up?
He saw that software development was swollowing the world.
After reading about it he read that in a few years most businesses would want coders.
The net income for the tech industry was 3x more than banking.
He then made one of the toughest decisions of his life, to become a software developer and leave the finacial side.
